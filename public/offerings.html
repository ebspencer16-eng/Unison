Inside the exercises

Built for depth, not just breadth

Every dimension and category was chosen because it actually matters — not because it's easy to measure.

Exercise 01

8 Communication Dimensions

Five questions per dimension, calibrated to reveal genuine orientation rather than aspiration.

  • Energy Style — how you each recharge
  • How You Decide — logic vs. intuition
  • Handling Hard Moments — pace and approach
  • Feeling Loved — how affection lands
  • Planning Style — structure vs. spontaneity
  • Expressiveness — how openly you share
  • Togetherness — closeness vs. independence
  • Handling Change — how you move through uncertainty

Methodology

Designed around how people actually are

Not what they wish they were, or what they think their partner wants to hear.

  • Independent completion keeps answers genuine
  • Results only unlock once both are done
  • No right or wrong — just your actual orientations
  • Feedback is descriptive, not prescriptive
  • Designed for couples who want to understand each other more deeply

Common questions

Everything you might want to know

Do we both have to complete it? +

Yes — that's intentional. The joint results only unlock once both partners are done. That's what keeps answers genuine and makes the comparison meaningful. You can start at different times; progress saves automatically.

Can my partner see my answers before they finish? +

No. Your individual answers stay completely private until both of you have finished both exercises. Neither partner can see the other's responses until results unlock.

Is this couples therapy? +

No. Attune is a self-guided tool for understanding — not treatment. It's designed for couples who are already good and want to be great. If you're navigating serious challenges, a licensed therapist is the right resource. (Our Premium package includes a one-time LMFT session as a complement to the exercises — not as a substitute for ongoing therapy.)

What if our results show we're very different? +

Differences aren't problems — they're information. Attune is designed to explain what your differences mean and how to navigate them, not to tell you what's good or bad about your pairing. Many couples with significant differences are deeply well-suited precisely because they understand each other clearly.

How does the LMFT session work in the Premium package? +

After purchase, you'll schedule your session through our therapist network. Your LMFT receives your joint results before the appointment and comes prepared with context specific to your pairing. The 60-minute session is for both partners together, conducted virtually. You'll receive post-session notes and personalized next steps afterward.

How do physical gift boxes work? +

You purchase the gift box and provide the recipient couple's shipping address. The box ships within 3–5 business days and includes everything they need to get started — the physical keepsake item, a printed QR card linking to their digital experience, and your personal gift message. The digital portion activates when they scan the QR code.

Can we redo the exercises later? +

Not yet, but it's coming. What each of you needs, values, and envisions shifts over time — and we're building a way to revisit your results periodically so you stay genuinely current with each other.
