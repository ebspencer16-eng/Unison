Getting started

Each partner completes two exercises independently — you don't see each other's answers while you're doing them. Once both partners have finished both exercises, your results unlock simultaneously. You'll see a side-by-side comparison of how you each answered, along with observations specific to your pairing. The whole thing takes about 25 minutes per person.

No — you each complete the exercises whenever you want, on your own. Your results stay locked until both of you have finished both exercises. You can do yours today and your partner can do theirs next week. There's no expiration on completing the exercises.

No. Your individual answers are completely private until both partners have completed both exercises. This is intentional — answering honestly is only possible when you know your partner isn't watching. Once both of you are done, everything unlocks together at the same moment.

Most people finish each exercise in 12–15 minutes, so both exercises together take about 25–30 minutes per person. There's no time pressure — you can pause and come back. Your answers are saved automatically as you go.

The exercises & results

Your results are a side-by-side comparison — not a score or compatibility percentage. You'll see how each of you answered across all 8 communication dimensions and 10 expectation categories, with observations written specifically for your combination. There are also conversation starters drawn from the areas where your answers reveal something worth exploring most. See our What's Included page for a full breakdown.
