How it works

The understanding that used to take years — in one afternoon.

Each of you answers independently. Your answers combine into something neither of you could see alone — a precise, personal understanding of how you each work, and what that means for your relationship.

Step one

Each partner answers on their own.

You each complete both exercises separately — on your own time, on your own device, without comparing notes. There's no right answer to aim for. Your partner can't see your responses until you're both done.

Takes about 25 minutes total, split across two exercises
Answer honestly — not how you think you should, or how your partner might want you to
You can pause and come back — progress saves automatically

Step two

Results lock until you're both done.

Once you finish, you'll see your partner's progress — but nothing more. Your joint results only unlock when both of you have completed both exercises. That's what keeps the answers genuine.

Neither partner can see the other's answers until both are complete
You'll get notified the moment your partner finishes
Invite your partner with a simple link — no app download needed

Who it's for

For couples who are already good — and want to be great.

Attune isn't a rescue tool. It's for couples who believe that understanding each other more deeply is one of the most worthwhile things they can invest in.

Starting out

Before assumptions have time to quietly take root. Understanding how you each think now is one of the most intentional things you can do for what you're building.

Going deeper

You know each other well, but there are things you've never quite named. Attune surfaces the conversations that feel vulnerable to start — in a structure that makes them easier.

Staying current

What each of you needs, values, and envisions can shift. Revisiting your results periodically is how couples stay genuinely current with each other.
