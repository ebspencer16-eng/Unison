What's included

Two exercises. One complete picture of who you are together.

Each exercise covers different territory. Together they give you a shared language and a real understanding of how you each think, what you each expect, and where you naturally align — or don't.

Exercise 01

Communication Style

Before you can understand each other, you need to understand yourselves. Exercise 1 maps how each of you naturally operates across 8 dimensions — your energy, your emotional wiring, how love lands for you, how you handle stress. The things you might know in your gut but have never quite put words to.

⚡

Energy & Recharge

How you restore — alone or with people. Whether you seek stimulation or quiet. What drains you without you realizing.

🧠

Decision-Making

How you process choices — logic-first or feeling-first. How you handle uncertainty. What good decisions feel like to you.

💬

Conflict Style

What you do when tension rises — do you engage, withdraw, or defer? What you need to feel heard during hard conversations.

❤️

How Love Lands

What makes you feel genuinely cared for — and what your partner might be doing that misses the mark without knowing.

🗂️

Structure & Flexibility

Your preference for plans vs. openness. How you handle schedules, spontaneity, and change.

🌊

Emotional Expression

How much you share, how quickly you open up, and how you process emotion — internally or outwardly.

🤝

Closeness & Independence

How much togetherness vs. space you need. What feels like connection vs. what feels like pressure.

🔮

Future Orientation

Whether you live in the present or plan ahead. How you think about long-term goals and what "building a life" means to you.

Sample questions from Exercise 1

Energy & Recharge

After a long week, what sounds most restorative — a quiet night at home, or plans with people you care about?

Conflict Style

When you're upset about something in the relationship, what do you tend to do first — bring it up right away, take space to process, or let it go?

How Love Lands

What does it feel like when someone truly shows up for you? What do they do?

Decision-Making

When facing a big decision, do you tend to rely more on data and logic, or on how something feels?

Each partner answers independently before seeing the other's responses. About 12–15 minutes.

Exercise 02

Shared Expectations

Most relationship friction comes from expectations that were never discussed — not because either person is wrong, but because they were never surfaced. Exercise 2 covers the practical and emotional terrain that quietly shapes everything: money, family, how you handle your home, closeness, ambition. 10 categories, answered honestly, compared side by side.

💰

Finances

How you think about money — shared vs. separate, saving vs. spending, financial goals and who leads financial decisions.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family & Children

Your expectations around family planning, parenting styles, extended family involvement, and the role of family in your life.

🏠

Home & Daily Life

How you want your home to feel, division of household responsibilities, cleanliness standards, and daily rhythms.

💼

Careers & Ambition

How much your career defines you, how you'd handle work-life imbalances, and what professional support looks like in a relationship.

🌍

Lifestyle & Values

Religion, politics, social life, how you spend free time, and what kind of life you're building toward.

🤲

Closeness & Intimacy

What closeness means to each of you — emotional, physical, and how needs in this area can shift over time.

🎯

Long-Term Vision

Where you see yourself in 10 years, what "a good life" looks like, and whether your visions are aimed at the same horizon.

🧘

Health & Wellbeing

Your approach to physical and mental health, how you support each other through hard times, and what health means in daily life.

Sample questions from Exercise 2

Finances

In an ideal partnership, would you prefer fully merged finances, a hybrid approach, or largely separate accounts?

Family & Children

How important is it to you that your extended family plays an active role in your daily life or major decisions?

Careers & Ambition

If your career asked you to relocate or significantly change your schedule, how would you weigh that against your relationship needs?

Long-Term Vision

When you picture a really good life 10 years from now, what does it look like? Where are you, and what does a typical week feel like?

Both exercises must be complete before results are revealed. Neither partner sees the other's answers until the joint view unlocks. About 10–12 minutes.

What you'll see

Your results are a side-by-side picture, not a score.

No compatibility percentage. No types that label you. What you get is a clear comparison of how each of you actually answered — so you can see where you're aligned, where you differ, and what's worth a real conversation.

Communication Overview

Your individual profiles

A summary of how each of you tends to show up across all 8 communication dimensions — your patterns, strengths, and the things worth understanding about each other.

Side-by-Side

Dimension by dimension

Each of the 8 dimensions shown with both your answers visible — where you align, where you're different, and what that difference actually means for you two.

Expectations

What you each expect

All 10 expectation areas shown together. The gaps are named clearly — not as problems, but as the conversations worth having while they're still easy.

Joint Insights

What this means for you two

Observations written from your actual combination of answers — not generic advice. What your specific pairing looks like, where your natural strengths are, and what to watch for.

Conversation Starters

Where to begin

3–5 specific prompts built from your biggest gaps — framed to open a productive conversation rather than a defensive one.

Yours to keep

No expiration

Your results live in your portal. One purchase, yours permanently. Come back anytime — and add a Personalized Workbook to take the work further.

Ready to see how you actually fit together?

Two exercises. About 25 minutes each. Everything you learn is yours to keep.
